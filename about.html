a constant exploration of personal style and colour

Through my personal study, cognition and understanding, I have developed my own color palette style. Even though sometimes they may look strange because they are not traditional colors, I still hope to keep exploring. In terms of themes, I prefer to create some conceptual illustrations. For instance, I am very fond of animals and nature, so I hope to call on people to protect the Earth through some of my works.

a lifelong fascination

Since I was a child, I have been very fond of drawing. At that time, my works were pure and lovely. As I grew up, more and more complex thoughts emerged in my mind. I often think that if my works are merely for decoration, then their significance would only be beauty and decoration. This is not what I want. Just like I enjoy writing essays, I care about what messages and emotions my works can convey to people. My heart is passionate, but I can't express it. So I hope my works can help me convey it.

My story

Since I was a child, I have had a wide range of hobbies, such as reading, writing, animals, swimming, mountain climbing, traveling, playing badminton, music and so on. I think art is not merely about depicting things in reality realistically, because cameras can do that too. Rather, it is more about feeling nature with your heart. Exploring many hobbies is not a waste of time, but rather an increase in inspiration and a enrichment of the spirituality of my works, so that when people see my works, they will find them very vivid and want to convey something.
